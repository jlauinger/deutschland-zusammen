This is "United Help":

With this platform we would like to bring people together. Those who are looking for help and those who can offer some. In times of crisis it is especially important that we show solidarity.

If you need help, enter your street and town (e.g. Fifth Avenue, New York City) as well as the date for which you are searching into the fields above. We will then find people close by who are happy to help.

We have already found helpful people offering help for the following tasks:

  • Grocery shopping
  • Walking a pet
  • Picking up medicine
  • Dropping off mail
  • Courier services

Search for help now

This is how it works:

Are you able and willing to help your neighbor with e.g. grocery shopping, walking their pet, getting medicine for them, or any other thing you might think of? Great!

  1. You create a profile and tell us where you live and when and what kind of help you can offer. You also give us a radius around your location wherein you can reach people looking for help. Don't worry, your address will never be shown publicly.
  2. When somebody finds your profile, they can send you a message directly through unitedhelpusa.com. You receive it by e-mail and into your profile. We can also send you a push notification directly to your phone.

    You can even put your phone number into your profile so people can reach you even faster, but remember that it is public then. We have put together some important advice about how to secure your privacy and safety here.
  3. You agree on a date and time to help your neighbour.

You would like to help? Create your account now. Together we are stronger than the virus!
